Starbucks Origins

  • Started in 1971, Pike Place Market. Seatle, WA
  • Starbucks got its name from classic nautical book Moby Dick, the white whale hunting. The original Starbucks twin-taled Siren logo was designed to reflect the seductive imagery of the sea
  • Howard Schultz bought the original store in 1987, Since then, Starbucks has added an average of 2 stores a day

Starbucks Company

  • Today Starbucks is the largest coffeehouse company in the world, with 27,339 retail stores and more than 300,000 employess in 55 countries
  • So popular in China, a new store opens every 15 hours
  • More than 14,000 stores and more than 191,000 employees in US territory
  • Earns $24,72 billion worldwide and owns 39.8% share of US Coffee Market
  • Within the past ten years Starbucks has had a great revenue increase of 9.90 percent
  • There are more than 300,000+ Starbucks Employees in the world, of those, 191,000 are in US
